BATTLEFIELD SYSTEM OPERATIONS GUIDE

1. FLEET DEPLOYMENT PHASE

Select a ship from the left docking panel. Click inside the left **FRIENDLY FLEET** grid. Press **R** or click **ROTATE** to change alignment between horizontal and vertical. Press **AUTO PLACE** to let the AI align your fleet instantly.

2. COMBAT & FIRING CONTROLS

Once battles are initiated, click inside the right **HOSTILE SECTOR** to deploy missiles. Hits are marked in **RED** and misses are marked in **BLUE**. The AI acts immediately after your turn.

3. CYBERPUNK WEAPONS PROTOCOLS

Activate special skills below the enemy grid (1x per match):

  • SONAR RADAR: Scans a 3x3 block sector, temporarily highlighting ship locations in yellow for 3s without taking a shot.
  • CLUSTER STRIKE: Detonates a 5-cell cross pattern centered at target coordinate.

4. AUDIO ENGINE

Adjust master sound levels via the slider in the header. Background music plays the custom soundtrack HighPressure.mp3, and sound FX are generated on-the-fly via Web Audio API.
