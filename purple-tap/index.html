PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: June 3, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our game collects, uses, and safeguards your local data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

1. Data Collection & Local Storage

We use standard HTML5 local storage (localStorage) to store player profiles on your device. This includes your high score, game frames, total collected chips, XP progression, level, equipped ninja skins, and daily reward streak timestamps. This data remains on your physical device unless you clear your browser cache.

2. Google Account Linking

Linking your guest account to a Google Profile enables Neonja Cloud Backup synchronization. We only store a verified reference to your email address (e.g. user@gmail.com) to allow restoring progress across multiple browsers or devices. We do not access your contacts, passwords, or personal files.

3. Cookies Usage

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. We only store essential state flags to remember whether you have accepted our cookie policy to avoid displaying the notice repeatedly.

4. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have full control over your data. At any time, you can reset or delete your profile. To perform a complete data deletion, go to the Shop or Profile panel and click "Disconnect" or reset your browser cache. This instantly destroys all local and synced profile records.

5. Contact & Support

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, you can consult the development logs or report issues on our GitHub repository: github.com/ALATA2/INDIEstore.
