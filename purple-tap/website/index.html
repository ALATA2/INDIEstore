Chrono-Stealth & Lost Memories: Enter the Sesiavalley Grid

NEONJA: CHRONO SHIFT is not just a high-speed cyberpunk action runner—it is a journey through a fragmented digital history.

Step into a digitized memory. In this fast-paced PC indie game, one of the most critical sectors takes players on an immersive journey through a reconstructed virtual simulation of real-world landscapes. Recognized as a highly anticipated videogioco indie, NEONJA blends history and sci-fi.

Players will race through the neon-drenched remnants of the Borgosesia simulation sector, a virtual city rebuilt from corrupted architectural archives. Dash across the digital riverbeds of Valsesia, where the flowing streams have been replaced by rushing channels of high-velocity data code. The entire region, known in-game as Sesiavalley, represents a sanctuary of memories floating in the digital ether.

Deep within the heart of the network lies the Finocchito AI-Core—not just a database landmark, but a colossal, rogue security bastion. Safeguarding the central memory vaults, this Finocchito sentinel blocks your path with sweeping laser grids and gravity-altering fields. Defeating it is the only way to extract the Chrono core and unlock the ultimate ninja trails.
